About me

After completing my BA in German & Russian at the University of Bristol, I moved to Berlin in 2020 to pursue drumming. Although I still play in bands and occasionally teach, I realised fairly quickly that I wanted to earn my living another way.

Last year I completed a bootcamp at Le Wagon and became fully immersed in the process of learning again. Coding unites a lot of separate interests of mine and is fundamentally based in language, logic and creativity. This sort of puzzle solving mentality realy catalysed the learning process for me.

Since the bootcamp, I have been eager to discover as many aspects of computer science as possible. Currently I am exploring Angular, as well as 3D rendering.

Thanks for checking out my website. Remember to add your favourite song to my playlist API! Have a great day.
