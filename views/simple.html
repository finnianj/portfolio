About me

Hi there!

My name is Finn. I'm a creative software developer from the UK. I'm 27.

Last year I completed a coding bootcamp and became immersed in the process of learning a new skill. Coding unites many of my interests; it is fundamentally based in language, logic and creativity, and this sort of puzzle solving mentality really catalysed my curiosity.

After completing my BA in German & Russian at the University of Bristol, I moved to Berlin in 2020 to pursue drumming. Although I still play in bands and occasionally teach, I prefer to do it non-professionally.

Now I am eager to discover as many aspects of computer science as possible. Currently I am exploring Angular and Three.js, but I am always ready for something new.

Thanks for checking out my website. Remember to add your favourite song to my playlist API! Have a great day.
